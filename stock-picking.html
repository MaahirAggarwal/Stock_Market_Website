Stock Picking Techniques

To pick winning stocks, investors must combine technical and fundamental analysis. Here are a few popular strategies:

  • Value Investing: Focuses on stocks that are undervalued relative to their intrinsic value.
  • Growth Investing: Focuses on companies that exhibit signs of above-average growth.
  • Dividend Investing: Focuses on stocks that provide consistent dividends, generating a steady income stream.
